STRUCTURES

Structures are pre-generated buildings or formations found throughout the Minecraft world. They can range from simple villages and temples to towering bastions and fortresses. These structures often contain valuable and unique loot, as well as mobs that can pose a challenge to players. It is important for players to know about these structures, as some of these often contain essential resources as well as loot that can boost progression.

OVERWORLD

Due to the Overworld being similar to that of our own Earth, there are a multitude of structures to be found, that which cannot all be specified. However, they can be classified into different groups: underground, aboveground, and underwater structures. Underground structures include any structures that can only generate underground, such as strongholds or ancient cities, while aboveground structures, like jungle and desert temples, only generate on the surface of the Overworld. Lastly are underwater structures, like shipwrecks or ocean monuments, can only spawn underwater.

NETHER

Though the Nether is just as expansive as the Overworld, it has far fewer structures; only two, in fact: First is the Bastion Remnant. These humongous structures are ruins of citadels made of blackstone, filled with piglins and piglin brutes. These mobs guard the resources that hide within the remains of these structures, including but not limited to diamond armor and tools, gold, and a very ancient ore. Second is the Nether Fortress. These are towering castles made from nether brick, housing tall wither skeletons and blazes. In contrast to the Bastion Remnants, the value of these fortresses lay not in the loot found within, but the mobs that walk its paths. Wither skeletons and blazes drop valuable loot that is essential to find two of three great creatures.

THE END

With the End's desolate landscape, there is only one type of structure to be found here, and they are scattered few and far between. The End cities are tall structures found in the End islands. The abandoned cities are infested with shulkers, and inside them is scattered loot left by those who built this city. These structures may also be accompanied with a great ship called the End Ship, containing the head of the past Ender Dragons, and the wings that grant you true freedom; the elytra.
