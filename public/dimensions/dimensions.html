WHAT IS BLOCK BY BLOCK?

Block by Block is a project dedicated to helping the playerbase of Minecraft navigate its 3D world and explore its many dimensions, aiming to provide a comprehensive guide on the game's core mechanics and features. Through Block by Block, our team aims to help players utilize Minecraft's endless possibilities and creative freedom to its fullest potential.

DIMENSIONS

In the 3D world of Minecraft, there are realms called dimensions. These dimensions contain biomes, structures, mobs, and more, all unique to their respective realms. From the lively nature of the Overworld to the fiery hellscape of the Nether and the mysterious, empty expanse of the End, each dimension is unique in its own way, offering players different experiences as they explore the world.

THE NETHER

The Nether is an environment completely different to that of the Overworld. Likened to the underworld, it is a fiery hellscape containing an ocean of lava, with its ground consisting mainly of netherrack. Water evaporates in this biome, and there is not much life; the creatures that do exist are all either hostile or attack when threatened. In the Nether, rest is not an uption. You must traverse the hellish landscape in order to gather unique resources, and you must face formidable foes in the process. If you wish to obtain the ancient ores, or slay the fearsome dragon that sleeps in the End, you must venture into the Nether.

THE OVERWORLD

The Overworld is an environment very similar to that of our own Earth. With 54 distinct biomes, its landscape varies a lot from place to place. Teeming with life, the Overworld is home to all passive mobs, as well as many neutral and hostile mobs. It is the dimension that you spawn in when you first load the map, and it contains all that you need to survive and thrive.

THE END

Unlike the other two dimensions, the End is a barren, empty space. Surrounded by void, the End is like an ocean of void with islands of endstone scattered about. The sky is perpetually dark, with no sun or moon visible. The only mobs that spawn here are Endermen, and the only life that spawns are the tall trees of chorus plant.
